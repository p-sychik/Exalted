# EXALTED: Build Your Character
This WIP app will allow for a user to develop their skills in the real world to become a master of life.
The app will:
- Implement a wide array of skills
- Have a levelling/XP system that is consistently rewarding;
- Be easy to log;
    - It will have the ability to add to-dos with difficulty
        - On completion, the user optionally has the ability to add a message to their timeline
- Intend to be used over a prolonged period of time (typically years)
- Have a timeline showcasing all the achievements of the user, displaying the biggest achievement depending on timeline shown
- Exports of data available

Types of skills that will have experience to develop on:
**Alchemy:** A skill that focuses on developing your cooking skills.
**Craftsmanship:** Focuses on the creation of own work, could be creative work, physical, or any sort of creative component.
**Scholarship:** Focuses on reading and developing your knowledge, and understanding the world around you.
**Vigor:** Demonstrates improvement of physical strength and endurance.
**Eloquence:** The improvement of social skills, presence, articulation of thoughts and grace.
